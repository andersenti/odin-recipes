Pumpkin Bread

Description

a typical sweet quick bread, similar to banana bread or cranberry nut bread, in that it is leavened with baking powder and/or baking soda instead of yeast. The recipe can also be used to make muffins. A great fall snack.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • salt
  • baking soda
  • ground cloves
  • ground cinnamon
  • butter
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • pumpkin

Steps

  1. Preaheat oven to 325F
  2. Grease two 8 x 4 inch pans and dust with flour
  3. Combine in a medium bowl:
    • flour
    • salt
    • baking soda
    • baking powder
    • cloves
    • cinnamon
    • nutmeg
  4. In large bowl with electric mixer beat butter and sugar unil just blended.
  5. Add eggs and beat untli fluffy.
  6. Add flour mixture and mix slowly.
  7. Dispens batter to pans evenly.
  8. Bake for 65-75 minutes.
