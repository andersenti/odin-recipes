Chicken Marsala

Description

An Italian-American dish of chicken escalopes in a Marsala wine sauce. Chicken cutlets are tenderized and then pan-fried and warmed for later. Mushrooms are then fried in butter, with shallots and garlic added a short time into cooking. The sauce is now made by reducing the sauce of broth,wine, heavy cream and seasonings.

There are two ways this can be finished. While the sauce is still thin, the chicken can be added to be warmed up while the sauce thickens. Alternatively if your chicken has cooked through, the sauce can stay to thicken which will then be served over the chicken just before serving.

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts
  • flour
  • button mushrooms
  • shallots
  • garlic
  • chiken broth
  • Marsala wine
  • heavy cream
  • butter

Steps

  1. Tenderize chicken then coat with flower and spices
  2. Pan-fry chicken lightly, remove from pan and place in oven to warm
  3. Add butter and mushrooms to pan
  4. Early while cooking add garlic and shallots
    5. When done add broth, wine, cream and more spice
  5. Bring to boil then reduce heat for sauce to reduce and thicken
  6. When fully thickened server directly over the now fully-cooked chicken
