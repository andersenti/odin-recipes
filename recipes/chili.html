Chili

Description

A ground beef and tomato based stew with many varieties available. Pan fried meats turns to a slow-cooked hearty stew.

Ingredients

  • yellow onion
  • ground beef
  • tomato paste
  • beef broth
  • diced tomatoes
  • kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • chili powder

Steps
  1. In a large pot, fry onion and ground beef until browned.
  2. Add cumin, chili powder,garlic powder and tomato paste.
  3. Add broth, diced tomatoes and beans.
  4. Bring to boil then let simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Let sit 5-10 minutes before serving.
